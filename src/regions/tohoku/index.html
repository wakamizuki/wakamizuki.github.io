Discover Rural Tohoku: Nature, Culture, and Hot Springs

Tohoku is a region where rugged nature meets rich tradition! Explore majestic mountains, soak in historic hot springs, and experience vibrant festivals and hearty local cuisine. From the mystical Oirase Gorge to the snow monsters of Zao, Tohoku’s countryside is full of adventure and authentic rural life.

Explore Tohoku Countryside

Tohoku Countryside Highlights

  • Oirase Gorge (Aomori): Lush forests, waterfalls, and scenic hiking trails.
  • Yamadera (Yamagata): Historic temple perched on a mountainside.
  • Zao (Miyagi/Yamagata): Famous for snow monsters and hot springs.
  • Lake Tazawa (Akita): Japan’s deepest lake with stunning views.
  • Hirosaki Castle (Aomori): Cherry blossoms and samurai history.
  • Matsushima (Miyagi): One of Japan’s three scenic views, dotted with pine-clad islands.
  • Festivals: Nebuta, Kanto, and Tanabata—Tohoku’s vibrant summer celebrations.

Why Visit Tohoku’s Countryside?

Escape the crowds and discover a different side of Japan. Tohoku’s rural areas offer breathtaking landscapes, unique traditions, and warm hospitality—perfect for travelers seeking authentic experiences beyond the city.

Discover Rural Kinki: Temples, Mountains, and Local Life

Kinki is a region where tradition meets natural beauty! Explore the ancient temples of Kyoto and Nara, wander through the lively streets of Osaka, and hike the scenic mountains of Wakayama. From the spiritual Kumano Kodo pilgrimage routes to the picturesque cherry blossoms of Yoshino, Kinki’s countryside is full of cultural treasures and authentic experiences.

Explore Kinki Countryside

Kinki Countryside Highlights

  • Kyoto: World-famous temples, shrines, and traditional streetscapes.
  • Nara: Ancient capital with the Great Buddha and friendly deer in Nara Park.
  • Osaka: Vibrant city life, historic castles, and delicious street food.
  • Kumano Kodo (Wakayama): Sacred pilgrimage trails through lush mountains.
  • Yoshino (Nara): Renowned for spectacular cherry blossoms and mountain scenery.
  • Lake Biwa (Shiga): Japan’s largest lake with beautiful rural surroundings.

Why Visit Kinki’s Countryside?

Escape the crowds and discover a slower side of Japan. Kinki’s rural areas offer spiritual heritage, breathtaking nature, and warm hospitality—perfect for travelers seeking authentic experiences beyond the city.

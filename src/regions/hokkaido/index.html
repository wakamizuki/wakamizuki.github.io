Discover Rural Hokkaido: Nature, Culture, and Hot Springs

Hokkaido is Japan’s northern frontier, where vast wilderness meets rich culture! Explore dramatic national parks, soak in soothing hot springs, and experience unique festivals and fresh local cuisine. From the flower fields of Furano to the wild Shiretoko Peninsula, Hokkaido’s countryside is full of adventure and authentic rural life.

Hokkaido Countryside Highlights

  • Furano & Biei: Rolling hills, colorful flower fields, and scenic landscapes.
  • Shiretoko Peninsula: UNESCO World Heritage wilderness, wildlife, and waterfalls.
  • Noboribetsu Onsen: Famous hot spring town with dramatic “Hell Valley.”
  • Lake Toya: Caldera lake with beautiful views and fireworks in summer.
  • Otaru: Charming canal town with historic warehouses and fresh seafood.
  • Asahiyama Zoo: Unique animal exhibits and winter penguin parade.
  • Festivals: Sapporo Snow Festival, Yosakoi Soran, and more—Hokkaido’s vibrant celebrations.

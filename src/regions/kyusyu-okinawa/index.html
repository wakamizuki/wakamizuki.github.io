Discover Rural Kyushu & Okinawa: Volcanoes, Beaches, and Island Culture

Kyushu and Okinawa are regions where dramatic nature meets rich tradition! Explore the volcanic peaks of Aso and Sakurajima, relax on the tropical beaches of Okinawa, and experience vibrant festivals and unique local cuisine. From the historic hot springs of Beppu to the coral reefs of Ishigaki, Kyushu & Okinawa’s countryside is full of adventure and authentic island life.

Explore Kyushu & Okinawa Countryside

Kyushu & Okinawa Countryside Highlights

  • Kumamoto (Aso): Majestic volcanic landscapes and lush caldera scenery.
  • Kagoshima (Sakurajima): Active volcano and beautiful bay views.
  • Okinawa Main Island: White sand beaches, Ryukyu culture, and Shurijo Castle.
  • Yakushima: Ancient cedar forests and UNESCO World Heritage nature.
  • Beppu & Yufuin (Oita): Famous hot springs and relaxing countryside towns.
  • Ishigaki & Miyako Islands: Crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and island hospitality.

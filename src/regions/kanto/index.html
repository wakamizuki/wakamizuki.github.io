Discover Rural Kanto: Nature, Villages, and Local Life

Kanto isn’t just Tokyo! Venture out to find peaceful lakes, tea fields, hot springs, and mountain villages. From the iconic views of Mt. Fuji in Yamanashi to the relaxing onsen of Hakone and the tea culture of Saitama, Kanto’s countryside is full of hidden gems and authentic experiences.

Explore Kanto Countryside

Kanto Countryside Highlights

  • Oshino Village (Yamanashi): Thatched-roof houses, Mt. Fuji spring water ponds, and tranquil scenery. Oshino Guide
  • Hakone (Kanagawa): Famous for hot springs, Lake Ashi, art museums, and mountain views. Hakone Guide
  • Sayama & Tokorozawa (Saitama): Tea fields, hands-on tea experiences, and local parks. Sayama Model Trip
  • Chichibu (Saitama): Scenic valleys, historic shrines, and seasonal festivals.
  • Nikko (Tochigi): UNESCO shrines, waterfalls, and mountain hiking.
  • Gunma & Ibaraki: Onsen towns, flower parks, and rural landscapes.

Why Visit Kanto’s Countryside?

Escape the crowds and discover a slower side of Japan. Kanto’s rural areas offer stunning nature, traditional culture, and warm hospitality—perfect for travelers seeking authentic experiences beyond the city.

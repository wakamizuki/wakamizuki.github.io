Oshino Shinobi no Sato

Overview

Oshino Shinobi no Sato is a ninja-themed park located at the base of Mt. Fuji, especially popular with families and visitors interested in ninja culture. This unique park offers a variety of activities where you can experience and enjoy the traditional lifestyle and skills of Japan’s ninjas, making it a must-visit destination for ninja enthusiasts.

Activity Information

  • Location: Oshino Village, Yamanashi Prefecture, Japan
  • Time: Open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Recommended Season: Spring and Autumn for the best weather and clear views of Mt. Fuji.
  • Notes: Be sure to check the weather forecast before visiting. Some outdoor activities may be affected by weather conditions.
  • Budget: Approximately ¥1,500 to ¥2,000 per person (excluding optional experiences like ninja shows or costume rental).

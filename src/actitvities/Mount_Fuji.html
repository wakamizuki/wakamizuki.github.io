Mount Fuji(Mt. Fuji)

Overview

Day 1: You start climbing from the 5th station in the afternoon and spend the night at a mountain hut. It usually takes about 4 to 6 hours to reach the hut.
Day 2: Early in the morning, you leave the hut and climb to the summit in about 2 to 3 hours. After enjoying the sunrise at the summit, it takes around 3 to 5 hours to descend.

Activity Information

  • Location: Mt. Fuji, Japan
  • Time: Climbing season is from early July to early September
  • Recommended Season: Mid-July to late August for the safest and most popular time to climb.
  • Notes: Be sure to check the weather forecast and prepare for sudden changes in temperature. High-altitude sickness can occur, so take breaks.
  • Budget: Approximately ¥10,000 to ¥20,000 per person, including transportation, accommodation at a mountain hut, and gear rental.

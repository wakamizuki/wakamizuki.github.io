Sagami Lake MORI MORI

Overview

Sagami Lake MORI MORI offers a variety of outdoor activities surrounded by beautiful nature. Enjoy exciting high-rope courses, ziplining through the trees, and camping in scenic spots. It's a perfect destination for those seeking adventure and family-friendly fun.

Activity Information

  • Location: Sagamiko, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan
  • Time: Open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (on weekends)
  • Recommended Season: Spring and Autumn for the best weather and beautiful natural scenery.
  • Notes: Be sure to check the weather forecast before visiting. Some activities may be affected by weather conditions.
  • Budget: Approximately ¥6,000 to ¥9,000 per person

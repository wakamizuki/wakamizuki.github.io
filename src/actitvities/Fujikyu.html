Fuji-Q Highland

Overview

Fuji-Q Highland is renowned for its thrilling attractions set against the stunning backdrop of Mount Fuji. It features world-famous roller coasters like "FUJIYAMA," "Do-Dodonpa," and "Takabisha," making it a must-visit for thrill-seekers. The park also offers family-friendly rides and character shows, catering to visitors of all ages.

Activity Information

  • Location: Fujiyoshida, Yamanashi Prefecture, Japan
  • Time: Open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Hours may vary by season)
  • Recommended Season: Spring and Autumn for pleasant weather and to avoid peak summer crowds.
  • Notes: Check the official website for current opening hours and ride maintenance schedules. Some attractions may be closed due to weather or maintenance.
  • Budget: Approximately ¥6,000 to ¥9,000 per person for admission, with additional costs for food, merchandise, and special attractions.

Sarugakyo Bungy

Overview

Sarugakyo Bungy is a thrilling bungee jumping experience located in the picturesque Gunma Prefecture, Japan. Nestled amidst lush greenery and flowing rivers, this adrenaline-pumping attraction offers a unique opportunity to soar through the sky and enjoy breathtaking views. Whether you're a seasoned bungee jumper or a first-timer seeking an adventure, Sarugakyo Bungy promises an unforgettable experience for all thrill-seekers.

Activity Information

  • Location: Gunma Prefecture, Japan
  • Opening Hours: Varies, check the official website for details
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn offer pleasant weather for bungee jumping
  • Notes: Participants must meet certain weight and health requirements. Safety precautions are strictly followed.
  • Price: Prices range from ¥5,000 to ¥10,000 depending on the package chosen

Belluna Dome - A multipurpose dome for baseball, events, and enjoying nature

Overview

Belluna Dome, also known as MetLife Dome in the past, is the home stadium of the Saitama Seibu Lions, located in Tokorozawa, Saitama, Japan. Known for its unique structure—partially open-air with a roof—it provides a distinct baseball experience that blends the indoors and outdoors. It offers not only thrilling baseball games but also seasonal events and scenic surroundings in Seibu-en area.

Access to Belluna Dome by TRAIN

For international visitors, taking the train is the most recommended way to reach Belluna Dome. Not only is it convenient and reliable, but it also allows you to enjoy a cold beer at the game without worrying about driving. Since the dome is located in Tokorozawa, about an hour from central Tokyo, taking a taxi would be quite expensive. The nearest station, Seibu-Kyujomae Station, is directly connected to the stadium, making train travel the easiest and most stress-free option.

Activity Information

  • Location: Tokorozawa, Saitama, Japan
  • Time: Game times vary; doors typically open 1-2 hours before games
  • Recommended Season: Spring to Autumn is ideal for enjoying both the games and the surrounding greenery.
  • Notes: Weekends and holidays tend to be crowded. Consider booking tickets in advance. The dome also hosts concerts and festivals.
  • Budget: Around ¥3,000 to ¥9,000 per person, depending on seat choice and event
  • Home Team: Saitama Seibu Lions (NPB – Pacific League)

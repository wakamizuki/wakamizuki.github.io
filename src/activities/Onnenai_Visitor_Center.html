Onnenai Visitor Center – Gateway to Kushiro Wetlands

Overview: Start Your Wetland Adventure Here

The Onnenai Visitor Center (温根内ビジターセンター) is the main entrance to the Onnenai area of Kushiro Shitsugen National Park. Here, you can get maps, learn about the marsh’s unique ecosystem, and prepare for your walk on the famous Onnenai Boardwalk. The center is barrier-free and offers a comfortable rest stop before or after exploring the wetlands.

What Can You Do at the Visitor Center?

The center features educational displays about the marsh’s plants, birds, and animals. Friendly staff provide information on the best walking routes and seasonal highlights. There’s a small shop for souvenirs and snacks, and clean restrooms are available.

  • Pick up free maps and walking guides
  • See displays about wetland wildlife and ecology
  • Ask staff for birdwatching tips and current conditions
  • Rest in the lounge area or shop for local goods
  • Use barrier-free restrooms and facilities

Local Legend: The Marsh’s Guardians

Locals say the spirits of the marsh watch over visitors from the reeds. The Visitor Center is said to be a place where people and nature meet in harmony, guided by these gentle guardians.

Local saying:
「ビジターセンターは湿原の心」
Translation:
"The Visitor Center is the heart of the marsh."

Visitor Center Details

The center is barrier-free and suitable for all ages. It’s the perfect place to learn about the marsh before heading out on the boardwalk. Staff can help with route planning and local tips.

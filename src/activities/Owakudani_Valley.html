Owakudani Valley

Overview

Owakudani Valley, located in Hakone, Japan, is a volcanic valley known for its sulfuric hot springs, unique black eggs cooked in the hot springs, and breathtaking views of Mount Fuji. Visitors can take a ropeway ride to reach the valley and enjoy the dramatic landscape created by volcanic activity.

Activity Information

  • Location: Hakone, Japan
  • Opening Hours: Daily, hours may vary
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn offer pleasant weather for exploring the valley.
  • Tips: Be prepared for strong sulfuric odors and fluctuating weather conditions. The black eggs are said to add seven years to your life.
  • Cost: Ropeway ticket prices vary; budget around ¥1,000 to ¥2,000 per person for the experience.

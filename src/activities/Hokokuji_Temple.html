Hokokuji Temple

Overview

Hokokuji Temple, located in Kamakura, Japan, is a tranquil Buddhist temple renowned for its serene bamboo forest. The temple offers visitors a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life, with the sound of rustling bamboo leaves creating a calming atmosphere. Apart from the bamboo grove, Hokokuji Temple features a traditional tea house where visitors can enjoy matcha tea surrounded by the beauty of nature.

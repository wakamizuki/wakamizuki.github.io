Hase Temple

Overview

Hase Temple, also known as Hasedera, is a historic Buddhist temple located in Kamakura, Japan. Renowned for its serene atmosphere, beautiful gardens, and stunning architecture, Hase Temple is a must-visit for those seeking cultural and spiritual experiences. Visitors can explore various halls, pagodas, and statues, including the iconic Kannon statue with eleven faces.

Activity Information

  • Location: Kamakura, Kanagawa, Japan
  • Opening Hours: 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn offer pleasant weather for exploring the temple and its surroundings.
  • Notes: Wear comfortable shoes as there are stairs on the temple grounds. Don't miss the panoramic view of Kamakura from the observation deck.
  • Entrance Fee: ¥300 for adults, ¥100 for children

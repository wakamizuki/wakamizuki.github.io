Tsurugaoka Hachimangu

Overview

Tsurugaoka Hachimangu is a historic Shinto shrine located in Kamakura, Japan. It holds significant cultural and religious importance and is known for its stunning architecture and beautiful surroundings. Visitors can explore the various buildings within the shrine complex, participate in traditional rituals, and witness cultural events that take place throughout the year.

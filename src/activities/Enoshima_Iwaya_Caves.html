Enoshima Iwaya Caves

Overview

Enoshima Iwaya Caves are natural caves located on the island of Enoshima in Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. These caves have a rich historical significance and feature stunning rock formations that attract visitors from around the world. Exploring the Enoshima Iwaya Caves offers a unique glimpse into the geological wonders of the region.

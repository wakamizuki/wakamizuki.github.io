Odawara Castle

Overview

Odawara Castle is a beautifully restored Japanese castle located in Odawara City, Kanagawa Prefecture. Once a major stronghold during the Sengoku period, it now stands as a symbol of history and culture. Visitors can explore the castle tower, samurai museum, and surrounding park filled with seasonal flowers. The site offers panoramic views of the city and Sagami Bay, making it a great spot for sightseeing and historical learning.

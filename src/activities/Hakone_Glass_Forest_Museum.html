Hakone Glass Forest Museum

Overview

The Hakone Glass Forest Museum is a captivating glass art museum located in the picturesque town of Hakone, Japan. This museum showcases a stunning collection of glass sculptures and artworks, set amidst beautifully manicured gardens and tranquil surroundings. Visitors can immerse themselves in the world of glass art and witness the intricate craftsmanship behind each masterpiece.

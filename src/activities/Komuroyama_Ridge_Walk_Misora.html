Komuroyama Ridge Walk Misora – Scenic Hiking in Izu

Overview: A Breathtaking Ridge Walk in Nature

Komuroyama Ridge Walk Misora offers a picturesque hiking trail with stunning views of the Izu Peninsula and Sagami Bay. The peaceful ambiance and natural beauty make it a perfect escape for nature lovers and hikers.

Example Local Bus Route from Ito Station:
  • Take the Tokai Bus (bound for Komuroyama Ridge Walk) from platform 6 at Ito Station.
  • Get off at Komuroyama Tsubaki-en (about 18 min, ¥510).
  • Walk about 7 minutes to the Komuroyama Ridge Walk Misora entrance.
  • Note: There is also a Tokai Bus from Ito Station bound for Shaboten Park. Get off at "Koen Chuo / Tokai Bus" (公園中央/東海バス) stop, which is closer to the Komuroyama Park central area.

Activity Information

  • Location: Ito, Shizuoka, Japan
  • Time: Open year-round, best during clear weather
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn for comfortable hiking temperatures
  • Notes: Wear comfortable hiking shoes and carry water. The trail may have some steep sections.
  • Cost: Free to access

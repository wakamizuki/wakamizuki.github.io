Atami Castle - A Historic Landmark with Scenic Views

Overview: Discover Atami’s Iconic Castle

Atami Castle, perched on a hilltop in Atami, Shizuoka, is a reconstructed castle offering sweeping views of the city and Sagami Bay. Although not an original Edo-period structure, it houses fascinating exhibits on samurai culture, armor, and Japanese history. The castle grounds feature lush gardens and seasonal flowers, making it a favorite spot for both history buffs and photographers.

How to Get to Atami Castle (Last updated: May 12, 2025)

From Atami Station, you can reach Atami Castle by taxi (about 10 minutes) or by local bus bound for Atami Castle or Atami Ropeway. Walking is possible but involves a steep uphill climb of about 30 minutes. The castle is well signposted from the station area.

Activity Information

  • Location: Atami, Shizuoka, Japan
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (may vary by season)
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn offer pleasant weather for exploring the castle and its surroundings.
  • Notes: The castle may be closed on certain holidays or for maintenance. Guided tours are available in multiple languages.
  • Admission: Entrance fee ranges from ¥500 to ¥1,200 per person.
    *If you wish to enjoy the Trick Art Museum, an additional fee is required.

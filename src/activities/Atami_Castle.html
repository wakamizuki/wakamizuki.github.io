Atami Castle

Overview

Atami Castle, situated in Atami, Shizuoka, Japan, is a historic castle known for its beautiful architecture and panoramic views of the surrounding area. Originally built in the Edo period, the castle has been reconstructed and restored over the years, offering visitors a glimpse into Japan's feudal past. Apart from the castle itself, visitors can enjoy the lush gardens and stunning vistas from the top of the castle tower.

Activity Information

  • Location: Atami, Shizuoka, Japan
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (may vary by season)
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn offer pleasant weather for exploring the castle and its surroundings.
  • Notes: The castle may be closed on certain holidays or for maintenance. Guided tours are available in multiple languages.
  • Admission: Entrance fee ranges from ¥500 to ¥1,000 per person.

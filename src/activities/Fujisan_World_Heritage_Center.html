Fujisan World Heritage Center

Overview

Fujisan World Heritage Center is a dedicated center showcasing the history, significance, and beauty of Mount Fuji, located in Fujinomiya, Shizuoka, Japan. The center offers visitors a comprehensive understanding of Mount Fuji's cultural and natural importance, along with stunning views of the iconic mountain.

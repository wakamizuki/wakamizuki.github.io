Kerama Islands

Overview

The Kerama Islands, located near Okinawa, are famous for their crystal-clear waters and colorful coral reefs, making them a top snorkeling destination in Japan. With great visibility and an abundance of marine life, including tropical fish and sea turtles, it's an ideal spot for both beginners and experienced snorkelers. The Kerama Islands provide an unforgettable snorkeling experience in a stunning natural setting.

Activity Information

  • Location: Kerama Islands, Okinawa Prefecture, Japan
  • Time: Open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (on weekends)
  • Recommended Season: Snorkeling is recommended in the summer. Sea turtles can be seen from May to August.
  • Notes: Always check weather conditions, as typhoons can impact sea activities. Bring reef-safe sunscreen to protect the delicate coral ecosystems.
  • Budget: Approximately ¥5,500 to ¥13,200 per person

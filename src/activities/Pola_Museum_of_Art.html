Pola Museum of Art

Overview

The Pola Museum of Art is a prestigious art museum nestled in the picturesque town of Hakone, Japan. Housing a remarkable collection of modern and contemporary art, the museum is known for its serene setting and stunning architecture. Visitors can marvel at works by renowned artists such as Monet, Picasso, and Warhol while enjoying the natural beauty that surrounds the museum.

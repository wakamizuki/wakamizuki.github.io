Hatsushima Island

Overview

Hatsushima Island is a picturesque island located off the coast of Atami in Japan. With its stunning landscapes, crystal-clear waters, and abundant marine life, it is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventurers.

Activity Information

  • Location: Off the coast of Atami, Japan
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring and Summer for ideal weather conditions
  • Activities: Snorkeling, diving, hiking, and beach relaxation
  • Tips: Plan your visit in advance, check for ferry schedules, and bring sunscreen and water.
  • Accommodation: Limited accommodations available on the island, consider staying in Atami.

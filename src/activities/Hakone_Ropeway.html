Hakone Ropeway

Overview

Hakone Ropeway is a must-visit tourist attraction in Hakone, Japan. It offers a scenic aerial ride that provides breathtaking views of Mount Fuji, Lake Ashi, and the surrounding Hakone National Park. The cable car journey is a memorable experience for visitors of all ages, combining natural beauty with a sense of adventure.

Activity Information

  • Location: Hakone, Japan
  • Operating Hours: Varies by season
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn offer clear views, but winter provides a snow-capped landscape.
  • Tips: Be prepared for crowds during peak seasons. Consider purchasing a Hakone Free Pass for convenient transportation around the area.
  • Cost: Ticket prices range from ¥1,000 to ¥2,000 per person for a round trip.

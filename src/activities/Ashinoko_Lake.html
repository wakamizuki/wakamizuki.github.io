Ashinoko Lake

Overview

Ashinoko Lake, or Lake Ashi, is a picturesque crater lake located in Hakone, Japan. Surrounded by lush greenery and with views of Mt. Fuji on clear days, it offers a serene escape from city life. Visitors can enjoy boat cruises on the lake, explore nearby hot springs, and take in the stunning natural beauty of the area.

Activity Information

  • Location: Hakone, Japan
  • Boat Cruises: Available throughout the day, offering views of the lake and surrounding mountains
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn are ideal for pleasant weather and stunning foliage
  • Notes: Don't miss the Hakone Shrine and Hakone Ropeway while visiting the area
  • Cost: Boat cruise tickets range from ¥1,000 to ¥2,000 per person

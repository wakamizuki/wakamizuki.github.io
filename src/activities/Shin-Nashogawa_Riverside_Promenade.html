Shin-Nashogawa Riverside Promenade

Overview

The Shin-Nashogawa Riverside Promenade offers a tranquil walking path along the Nashogawa River in Saitama, Japan. Visitors can enjoy the serene atmosphere and picturesque views of the river and surrounding greenery.

Activity Information

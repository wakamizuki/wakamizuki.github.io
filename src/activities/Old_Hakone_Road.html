Old Hakone Road

Overview

Old Hakone Road, also known as Kyu-Kaido, is a historic trail in Hakone, Japan, famous for its picturesque landscapes and cultural heritage. This ancient road has been traveled by pilgrims, samurais, and travelers for centuries, offering a glimpse into Japan's rich history.

Activity Information

  • Location: Hakone, Japan
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring and Autumn for pleasant weather
  • Highlights: Ancient cedar trees, stone markers, hot springs along the trail
  • Tips: Wear comfortable shoes, carry water and snacks for the hike

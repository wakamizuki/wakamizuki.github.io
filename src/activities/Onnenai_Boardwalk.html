Onnenai Boardwalk – Walk Through Kushiro Wetlands

Overview: The Gateway to Kushiro Marsh’s Heart

Onnenai Boardwalk (温根内木道) is a wooden path stretching deep into the Kushiro Shitsugen National Park. This easy, accessible trail lets you experience the marsh’s unique ecosystem up close—without getting your feet wet! The boardwalk is famous for wildflowers, birdwatching, and serene wetland views.

What Makes Onnenai Boardwalk Special?

The boardwalk is flat and well-maintained, making it suitable for all ages. In spring and summer, the marsh bursts with blooming flowers and singing birds. The Onnenai Visitor Center at the entrance offers maps, exhibits, and a place to rest before or after your walk.

  • Easy, barrier-free access to the marsh
  • Rich variety of wetland plants and flowers
  • Excellent birdwatching (look for red-crowned cranes!)
  • Peaceful, uncrowded nature experience
  • Visitor center with restrooms and displays

Local Legend: The Whispering Marsh

Locals say the marsh “whispers” on quiet mornings, as wind and water move through the reeds. Some believe these whispers are messages from the spirits of the wetlands, protecting the rare plants and animals that live here.

Local saying:
「湿原のささやきは自然の声」
Translation:
"The marsh’s whispers are the voice of nature."

Boardwalk Details

The main boardwalk loop is about 2km and takes 40–60 minutes at a relaxed pace. Side trails offer different views of the marsh. The path is flat and suitable for wheelchairs and strollers.

Hakone Mototsumiya Shrine

Overview

Hakone Mototsumiya Shrine is a historic Shinto shrine nestled in the picturesque Hakone region of Japan. It holds great cultural significance and offers a serene retreat for visitors seeking spiritual experiences amidst natural beauty. The shrine's architecture, surrounded by lush greenery and overlooking Lake Ashi, creates a tranquil atmosphere for contemplation and exploration.

Activity Information

  • Location: Hakone, Kanagawa, Japan
  • Opening Hours: Daily from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring for cherry blossoms, Autumn for fall foliage
  • Tips: Wear comfortable shoes for walking around the shrine grounds and bring a camera to capture the stunning views.

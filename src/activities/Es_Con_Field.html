Es Con Field Hokkaido

Overview

Es Con Field Hokkaido is a modern baseball stadium located in Kitahiroshima, Hokkaido, Japan. Opened in 2023, it serves as the home field for the Hokkaido Nippon-Ham Fighters, a professional baseball team. The stadium features a retractable roof, allowing for games in any weather, and is known for its cutting-edge facilities and eco-friendly design.

Activity Information

  • Location: Kitahiroshima, Hokkaido, Japan
  • Time: Game times vary; doors typically open 1-2 hours before games
  • Recommended Season: Summer and early Autumn, ideal for enjoying the stadium's open-roof feature and outdoor atmosphere.
  • Notes: Advance ticket purchase is recommended, especially for popular games. The stadium offers various food and beverage options showcasing Hokkaido specialties..
  • Budget: Approximately ¥4,000 to ¥10,000 per person, depending on seat selection and additional expenses

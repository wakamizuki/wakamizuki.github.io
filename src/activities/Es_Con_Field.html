Overview: Eco-Friendly & Innovative Baseball Stadium

Es Con Field Hokkaido is a state-of-the-art baseball stadium located in Kitahiroshima, Hokkaido, Japan. Opened in 2023, it is home to the Hokkaido Nippon-Ham Fighters. The stadium features a retractable roof, advanced facilities, and a design focused on sustainability and comfort for all visitors.

The easiest way to reach Es Con Field Hokkaido is by train. From Sapporo Station, take the JR Chitose Line to Kitahiroshima Station (about 16 minutes).

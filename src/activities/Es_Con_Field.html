Overview: Eco-Friendly & Innovative Baseball Stadium

Es Con Field Hokkaido is a state-of-the-art baseball stadium located in Kitahiroshima, Hokkaido, Japan. Opened in 2023, it is home to the Hokkaido Nippon-Ham Fighters. The stadium features a retractable roof, advanced facilities, and a design focused on sustainability and comfort for all visitors.

The easiest way to reach Es Con Field Hokkaido is by train. From Sapporo Station, take the JR Chitose Line to Kitahiroshima Station (about 16 minutes). Shuttle buses and taxis are available from Kitahiroshima Station to the stadium. Using public transportation is recommended, especially on event days.

For Those Who Want to Enjoy Nearby Nature

Lake Toya: Volcanic Beauty and Hot Springs

Lake Toya is a stunning caldera lake in Hokkaido, known for its crystal-clear waters, scenic islands, and surrounding hot spring resorts. Enjoy breathtaking views of Mount Usu, lakeside walks, and seasonal fireworks.

For Those Who Want to Enjoy Hot Springs

Noboribetsu Onsen Town: Hokkaido's Premier Hot Spring Resort

Noboribetsu Onsen Town is one of Hokkaido's most famous hot spring resorts, renowned for its variety of mineral-rich baths, dramatic Jigokudani (Hell Valley), and vibrant onsen culture. Visitors can enjoy relaxing soaks, scenic walking trails, and unique local cuisine.

Takachiho Gorge

Overview

Takachiho Gorge is a scenic natural wonder located in Takachiho, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan. It is known for its steep cliffs, lush greenery, and the Gokase River flowing through the gorge. Visitors can enjoy breathtaking views, take boat rides along the river, and explore nearby shrines and waterfalls.

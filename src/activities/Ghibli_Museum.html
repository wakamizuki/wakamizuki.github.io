GHIBLI MUSEUM

Overview

The GHIBLI MUSEUM, located in Mitaka, Tokyo, is dedicated to the enchanting world of Studio Ghibli's animated films. Visitors can explore various exhibitions showcasing the art and creativity behind beloved films like "My Neighbor Totoro" and "Spirited Away." The museum features exclusive short films, a charming café, and a shop filled with Ghibli merchandise. Reservations are required for entry, making it a must-visit destination for fans and families alike.

Activity Information

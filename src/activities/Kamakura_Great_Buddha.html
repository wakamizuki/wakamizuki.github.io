Kamakura Great Buddha

Overview

The Kamakura Great Buddha, also known as Daibutsu, is a monumental outdoor bronze statue of Amida Buddha located in Kamakura, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. Standing at over 13 meters tall, it dates back to the 13th century and is a significant cultural and religious symbol in Japan. Visitors can admire the serene beauty of the Great Buddha while exploring the historic city of Kamakura.

Activity Information

  • Location: Kamakura, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan
  • Visiting Hours: Open daily from 8:00 AM to 5:30 PM
  • Recommended Season: All year round, with cherry blossoms in spring and autumn foliage as popular times.
  • Notes: The site may be crowded during weekends and holidays. Respect the sacredness of the statue and the surrounding area.
  • Entrance Fee: ¥200 for adults, ¥150 for children

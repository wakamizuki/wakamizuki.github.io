Suzuhiro Kamaboko Museum

Overview

Suzuhiro Kamaboko Museum, located in Odawara, offers visitors an interactive experience focused on the traditional Japanese fish cake, kamaboko. Guests can learn about the history and production process through exhibits and enjoy hands-on workshops to create their own kamaboko. The museum also features a shop and tasting area where you can try a variety of fish cakes.

Activity Information

  • Location: Odawara, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan
  • Time: Open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (check for holidays)
  • Recommended Season: All year round, but spring and autumn offer the most pleasant weather.
  • Notes: Reservations are recommended for the kamaboko-making experience. The museum also features a shop and tasting area.
  • Budget: Approximately ¥1,000 to ¥3,000 per person, depending on activities.

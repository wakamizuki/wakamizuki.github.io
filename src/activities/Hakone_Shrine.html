Hakone Shrine

Overview

Hakone Shrine, located in Hakone, Japan, is a picturesque Shinto shrine surrounded by lush greenery and overlooking Lake Ashi. It has a long history and is known for its iconic red torii gate at the edge of the lake. Visitors can explore the peaceful grounds, take in the serene atmosphere, and enjoy stunning views of Mount Fuji on clear days.

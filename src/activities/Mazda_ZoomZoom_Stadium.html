Mazda Zoom-Zoom Stadium Hiroshima

Overview

Mazda Zoom-Zoom Stadium Hiroshima is the home stadium of the Hiroshima Toyo Carp, located in Hiroshima City, Japan. Known for its fan-friendly design and compact layout, the stadium offers a unique and exciting experience for baseball fans. It is one of Japan's most modern and innovative ballparks, providing great views from every seat. The stadium also hosts a variety of events, including concerts and community gatherings.

Activity Information

  • Location: Hiroshima City, Hiroshima, Japan
  • Time: Game times vary; doors typically open 1-2 hours before games
  • Recommended Season: Spring to Autumn is ideal for enjoying the games and the vibrant atmosphere.
  • Notes: Weekends and holidays tend to be crowded. Consider booking tickets in advance. The stadium also hosts concerts and events throughout the year.
  • Budget: Around ¥2,500 to ¥8,000 per person, depending on seat choice and event

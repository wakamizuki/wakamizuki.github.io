Mazda Zoom-Zoom Stadium Hiroshima - A modern ballpark for baseball, events, and local fun

Overview: A Fan-Friendly Stadium in Hiroshima

Mazda Zoom-Zoom Stadium Hiroshima is the home stadium of the Hiroshima Toyo Carp, located in Hiroshima City, Japan. Known for its open, fan-friendly design and compact layout, the stadium offers a unique and exciting experience for baseball fans. It is one of Japan's most modern and innovative ballparks, providing great views from every seat. The stadium also hosts a variety of events, including concerts and community gatherings.

For visitors, the easiest way to reach Mazda Zoom-Zoom Stadium is by walking from Hiroshima Station. You can also take a local bus or taxi, but walking is the most popular and convenient option.

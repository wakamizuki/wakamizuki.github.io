Zeniarai Benten Shrine

Overview

Zeniarai Benten Shrine is a unique shrine located in Kamakura, Japan, known for its ritual of washing money for good luck. This shrine holds great significance for believers seeking prosperity and wealth through this traditional practice. Visitors can experience the serene atmosphere, beautiful architecture, and the mystical charm of the shrine.

Activity Information

  • Location: Kamakura, Japan
  • Opening Hours: 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM
  • Recommended Time to Visit: Weekdays to avoid crowds
  • Notes: Follow the money washing ritual for good luck. Respect shrine customs and traditions.
  • Entry Fee: Free, but donations are appreciated

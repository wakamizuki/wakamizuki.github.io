Atami Sun Beach

Overview

Atami Sun Beach is a picturesque seaside destination located in Atami, Japan. This beautiful beach offers visitors a chance to relax under the sun, enjoy the sandy shores, and swim in the refreshing sea waters. Whether you're looking to sunbathe, take a dip in the ocean, or simply unwind by the shore, Atami Sun Beach provides the perfect setting for a day of leisure and enjoyment.

Activity Information

  • Location: Atami, Japan
  • Time: Open daily from morning to evening
  • Recommended Season: Summer is the best time to visit for beach activities and water sports.
  • Notes: Weekends and holidays can be crowded. Bring sunscreen, towels, and beach essentials. Various water sports and beach facilities are available.
  • Budget: Free entry to the beach, additional costs for rentals and activities.

Narusawa Ice Cave

Overview

Narusawa Ice Cave, located in Yamanashi Prefecture, Japan, is a fascinating natural ice cave that offers visitors a unique experience. The cave features stunning ice formations that have been formed over centuries, creating a surreal atmosphere for exploration. Visitors can learn about the history of the cave and marvel at the beauty of the intricate ice structures that make Narusawa Ice Cave a must-visit destination.

Activity Information

  • Location: Narusawa, Yamanashi Prefecture, Japan
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 4:30 PM (may vary by season)
  • Recommended Season: Winter is ideal to experience the full beauty of the ice formations
  • Notes: Dress warmly as the cave can be cold even in summer months. Guided tours are available.
  • Entry Fee: ¥600 for adults, ¥300 for children

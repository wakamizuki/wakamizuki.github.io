Kawazu Seven Waterfalls

Overview

Kawazu Seven Waterfalls is a series of seven stunning waterfalls located in Izu Peninsula, Japan. Each waterfall offers a unique and picturesque view, surrounded by lush greenery and a serene atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy hiking along the trails to witness the cascading beauty of these natural wonders.

Activity Information

  • Location: Izu Peninsula, Japan
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring for cherry blossoms or Autumn for fall foliage
  • Recommended Duration: Half-day to full day
  • Notes: Wear comfortable hiking shoes and bring water and snacks. Some trails may have steep sections.
  • Entrance Fee: Varies by waterfall, typically around ¥200 to ¥500 per person

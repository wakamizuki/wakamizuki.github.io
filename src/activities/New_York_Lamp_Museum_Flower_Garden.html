New York Lamp Museum Flower Garden

Overview

The New York Lamp Museum Flower Garden is a unique attraction located in the heart of New York City. It features a stunning collection of lamps and flowers, creating a magical and enchanting experience for visitors. Explore the history of lamps, enjoy the beauty of blooming flowers, and immerse yourself in this one-of-a-kind garden.

Activity Information

  • Location: New York City, New York, USA
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Closed on Mondays
  • Recommended Season: Spring and Summer are ideal for visiting to see the flowers in full bloom.
  • Notes: Tickets can be purchased online in advance. Guided tours are available.
  • Admission: $20 per person, discounts for seniors and children.

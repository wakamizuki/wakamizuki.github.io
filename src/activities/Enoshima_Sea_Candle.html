Enoshima Sea Candle

Overview

Enoshima Sea Candle, also known as Enoshima lighthouse, is a prominent landmark in Enoshima, Japan, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding area. Visitors can ascend the tower to enjoy the scenic vistas, explore the nearby Enoshima Island, and experience the enchanting coastal atmosphere.

Sengokuhara Susuki Grassland

Overview

Sengokuhara Susuki Grassland is a stunning natural attraction located in Hakone, Japan. Known for its vast fields of golden susuki grass swaying in the wind, it offers a serene and picturesque setting for visitors to enjoy. The grassland changes its colors with the seasons, with vibrant green in spring and golden hues in autumn, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Activity Information

  • Location: Hakone, Japan
  • Best Time to Visit: Autumn for golden susuki grass, Spring for fresh greenery
  • Recommended Activities: Nature walks, photography, picnic
  • Tips: Bring a camera, comfortable walking shoes, and snacks for a relaxing day in nature.

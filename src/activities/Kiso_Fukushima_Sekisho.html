Kiso Fukushima Sekisho - A Historical Checkpoint of the Edo Period

Overview: Step Back in Time Along the Nakasendo

Kiso Fukushima Sekisho served as an important checkpoint during the Edo period, located along the Nakasendo, a major route connecting Kyoto and Edo. This site was used to control the movement of people and goods. Today, it offers a glimpse into Japan's historical travel and trade practices.

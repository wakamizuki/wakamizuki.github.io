Kinomiya Shrine

Overview

Kinomiya Shrine, located in Atami, Shizuoka, Japan, is a historic shrine known for its sacred camphor tree that is over 2,000 years old. The shrine hosts traditional ceremonies and provides a serene atmosphere for visitors to experience Japanese spirituality. Visitors can explore the shrine grounds, make offerings, and participate in rituals to seek blessings and good fortune.

Activity Information

  • Location: Atami, Shizuoka, Japan
  • Visiting Hours: Shrine is open from morning until evening; specific ceremony times may vary
  • Recommended Time: Early morning or late afternoon for a peaceful experience
  • Notes: Respectful attire is recommended. Photography may be restricted in certain areas. Check for any ongoing events or festivals.
  • Cost: Admission to the shrine is free; donations are welcome

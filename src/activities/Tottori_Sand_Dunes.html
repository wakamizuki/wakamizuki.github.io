Tottori Sand Dunes

Overview

The Tottori Sand Dunes, located in Tottori, Japan, are a unique natural wonder stretching along the coast of the Sea of Japan. These expansive sandy landscapes offer visitors a mesmerizing experience of desert-like terrain in a coastal setting. The dunes are a popular tourist attraction, attracting visitors with their picturesque views, recreational activities, and opportunities for sandboarding and camel rides.

Activity Information

  • Location: Tottori, Japan
  • Time: Open throughout the year; best visited during the day for optimal views
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn are ideal for comfortable weather
  • Notes: Bring sunscreen, water, and comfortable footwear for exploring the dunes
  • Entry Fee: Admission fee applies; varies for different activities

Mt. Fuji Panoramic Ropeway

Overview

Mt. Fuji Panoramic Ropeway offers visitors an unforgettable experience with panoramic views of the majestic Mt. Fuji and its picturesque surroundings. The ropeway ride provides a unique perspective of the iconic mountain and is a must-do activity for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Activity Information

  • Location: Yamanashi Prefecture, Japan
  • Operating Hours: Varies depending on the season
  • Best Time to Visit: Clear days for optimal views of Mt. Fuji
  • Tips: Dress warmly as it can get chilly at higher altitudes. Check for any closures or maintenance schedules before your visit.
  • Cost: Prices range from ¥1,000 to ¥3,000 per person for a round trip

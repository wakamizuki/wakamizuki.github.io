Katase Higashihama Beach

Overview

Katase Higashihama Beach is a popular seaside destination in Fujisawa, Kanagawa, Japan. With its sandy shores, clear waters, and picturesque views, it offers a perfect setting for beachgoers and water sports enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy swimming, sunbathing, surfing, and beach volleyball at this vibrant coastal location. The beach is also known for its lively atmosphere, beachfront cafes, and stunning sunsets.

Activity Information

  • Location: Fujisawa, Kanagawa, Japan
  • Time: Open year-round, best enjoyed during the summer months
  • Recommended Season: Summer is peak season for beach activities and events.
  • Notes: Weekends can be crowded. Bring sunscreen, beachwear, and water sports gear if desired.
  • Cost: Free admission to the beach, fees may apply for water sports rentals.

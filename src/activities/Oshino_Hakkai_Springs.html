Oshino Hakkai Springs

Overview

Oshino Hakkai Springs is a group of eight natural spring ponds located in Oshino Village, Japan. These crystal clear ponds are fed by snow melt from Mount Fuji and have been a source of pure water for the local residents for centuries. The site holds historical significance and offers a serene atmosphere for visitors to enjoy the beauty of nature.

Activity Information

  • Location: Oshino Village, Yamanashi Prefecture, Japan
  • Visiting Hours: Open daily from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Recommended Season: Spring to Autumn for pleasant weather and clear views
  • Tips: Wear comfortable walking shoes as the site has pathways and stairs. Bring a camera to capture the picturesque scenery.
  • Entrance Fee: ¥300 for adults, ¥150 for children

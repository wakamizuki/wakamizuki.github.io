Lake Tazawa (Áî∞Ê≤¢Êπñ, Tazawa-ko) is a stunning caldera lake in Akita Prefecture, renowned for its deep blue water and mystical atmosphere. At 423.4 meters, it is the deepest lake in Japan. Lake Tazawa is perfect for nature lovers, families, and anyone seeking tranquility. Enjoy cycling around the lake (about 20km), take a sightseeing boat, or relax at lakeside cafes.

Fujiyama Adventure Club

Overview

Join us at Fujiyama Adventure Club for an exhilarating rafting experience on the beautiful and dynamic rivers of Japan! Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rafter, our tours provide the perfect balance of adventure, excitement, and safety. Our professional guides will take you through thrilling rapids while ensuring your safety throughout the trip. You'll be surrounded by breathtaking nature, with views of Mt. Fuji in the distance. It's an adventure perfect for families, friends, or solo travelers looking for a unique outdoor experience.

Activity Information

  • Location: Fujiyoshida, Yamanashi Prefecture, Japan
  • Time: Open from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (on weekends and public holidays)
  • Recommended Season: Spring and Autumn for mild weather and stunning views of Mt. Fuji.
  • Notes: Advanced reservations are recommended, especially on weekends. Some activities may have height or weight restrictions.
  • Budget: Approximately ¥7,000 to ¥10,000 per person, depending on the activity package chosen.

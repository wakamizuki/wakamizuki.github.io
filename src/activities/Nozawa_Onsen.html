Nozawa Onsen - A Traditional Hot Spring Village in Japan

Overview: A Perfect Blend of Culture and Nature

Nozawa Onsen is a charming village located in the Nagano Prefecture, famous for its natural hot springs and ski resorts. Visitors can enjoy a blend of traditional Japanese culture and modern winter sports. The village is home to numerous ryokans, offering a unique experience of Japanese hospitality and relaxation.

For international visitors, taking the train is the most convenient way to reach Nozawa Onsen. The journey from Tokyo involves taking the Hokuriku Shinkansen to Iiyama Station, followed by a short bus or taxi ride to the village. This route offers a comfortable and scenic trip through the Japanese countryside.

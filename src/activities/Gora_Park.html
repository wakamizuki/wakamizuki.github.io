Gora Park – Botanical Beauty in Hakone

Overview: Gardens, Flowers & Relaxation

Gora Park is a stunning botanical garden situated in the scenic town of Hakone, Japan. This picturesque park features lush greenery, colorful floral displays, and peaceful walking paths, making it a perfect retreat for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility.

Hakone Gora Park is perfectly located for exploring other top attractions in the Hakone area such as Hakone Open-Air Museum, Hakone Ropeway, and Hakone Shrine. Whether you're enjoying the artistic sculptures outdoors, taking in panoramic views from the ropeway, or soaking in the spiritual beauty of the lakeside shrine, the area offers a well-rounded mix of art, nature, and tradition — all easily accessible from Gora Park.

How to Get to Gora Park

  • From Hakone-Yumoto: Take the Hakone Tozan Railway to Gora Station (approx. 40 min). Gora Park is a 5-minute walk from the station.
  • From Odawara: Take the Hakone Tozan Railway to Gora Station.
  • From Tokyo: Take the Odakyu Romancecar to Hakone-Yumoto, then transfer to the Hakone Tozan Railway.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to Gora Park from Hakone-Yumoto?

Take the Hakone Tozan Railway from Hakone-Yumoto to Gora Station. Gora Park is a 5-minute walk from the station.

What is the best time to visit Gora Park?

Spring for cherry blossoms and roses, and autumn for colorful foliage.

Is there an admission fee for Gora Park?

Yes. Admission is ¥550 for adults and ¥250 for children.

Are there cafes or shops in Gora Park?

Yes, there is a cafe and a souvenir shop inside the park.

Gora Park

Overview

Gora Park is a stunning botanical garden situated in the scenic town of Hakone, Japan. This picturesque park features lush greenery, colorful floral displays, and peaceful walking paths, making it a perfect retreat for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility.

Park Information

  • Location: Hakone, Kanagawa, Japan
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (may vary seasonally)
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring for cherry blossoms, and autumn for colorful foliage
  • Notes: Admission fee required. Cafe and souvenir shop available on-site.
  • Entry Fee: ¥550 for adults, ¥250 for children

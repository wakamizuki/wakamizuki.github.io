Atsugi Nanasawa Forest Park

Overview

Atsugi Nanasawa Forest Park is a serene nature reserve located in Atsugi, Kanagawa, Japan. The park boasts lush greenery, tranquil hiking trails, and a peaceful atmosphere perfect for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy the beauty of the forest, relax by the ponds, and explore the various flora and fauna that call this park home.

Activity Information

  • Location: Atsugi, Kanagawa, Japan
  • Opening Hours: Park hours vary by season; check the website for details
  • Recommended Season: Spring and autumn offer pleasant weather for exploring the park's trails and enjoying the natural beauty.
  • Notes: Bring comfortable walking shoes and binoculars for birdwatching. Picnics are allowed in designated areas. Admission is typically free.
  • Cost: Free entry; some activities may require additional fees

Ashinoko Cruise

Overview

Ashinoko Cruise offers a scenic boat tour on Lake Ashi with stunning views of Mt. Fuji in the background. Experience the tranquility of the lake while enjoying the picturesque surroundings and the iconic volcano. The cruise provides a relaxing and memorable experience for visitors looking to explore the natural beauty of Hakone.

Activity Information

  • Location: Hakone, Kanagawa, Japan
  • Operating Hours: Daily departures; check schedule on the website
  • Recommended Time: Morning or late afternoon for the best views of Mt. Fuji
  • Tickets: Can be purchased online or at the ticket counter at the pier
  • Additional Info: Dress warmly as it can get chilly on the boat. Photography opportunities are abundant.

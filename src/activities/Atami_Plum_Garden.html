Atami Plum Garden

Overview

Atami Plum Garden is a serene garden located in Atami, Japan, known for its stunning plum blossoms. It offers a peaceful retreat for visitors to enjoy the beauty of nature and traditional Japanese landscaping. The garden features a variety of plum trees, winding paths, and picturesque views of the surrounding area, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Activity Information

  • Location: Atami, Shizuoka, Japan
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM daily
  • Best Time to Visit: Late winter to early spring during the plum blossom season
  • Notes: Weekdays are less crowded than weekends. Photography is allowed but be respectful of other visitors.
  • Entrance Fee: ¥500 for adults, ¥300 for children

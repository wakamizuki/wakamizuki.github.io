Atami Plum Garden – A Tranquil Retreat for Plum Blossoms and Nature

Overview: Experience Japan’s Earliest Plum Blossoms

Atami Plum Garden is a serene garden in Atami, Shizuoka, Japan, renowned for its early-blooming plum trees. The garden features over 400 plum trees of 60 varieties, winding paths, and picturesque landscapes, making it a favorite for nature lovers and photographers. The annual Plum Festival (Ume Matsuri) attracts visitors from across Japan, offering a blend of natural beauty and cultural events.

From Atami Station, take a local bus bound for "Baien" (Plum Garden) or a taxi (about 10 minutes). The garden is well signposted and easy to reach from the station.

Activity Information

  • Location: Atami, Shizuoka, Japan
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM daily
  • Best Time to Visit: Late winter to early spring (Jan–Mar)
  • Notes: Weekdays are less crowded. Photography is allowed. The garden is hilly; wear comfortable shoes.
  • Entrance Fee: ¥300 for adults, ¥0 for children

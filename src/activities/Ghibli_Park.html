GHIBLI PARK

Overview

GHIBLI PARK is a theme park located in Aichi Prefecture, Japan, dedicated to the enchanting worlds of Studio Ghibli films. Opened in 2022, it features immersive areas inspired by beloved movies like "My Neighbor Totoro," "Spirited Away," and "Howl's Moving Castle." Visitors can explore meticulously crafted environments, enjoy exhibitions, and experience the magic of Ghibli’s storytelling in a serene natural setting. It's a must-visit destination for fans of the studio's iconic animations.

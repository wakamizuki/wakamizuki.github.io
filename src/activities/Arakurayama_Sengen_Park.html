Arakurayama Sengen Park

Overview

Arakurayama Sengen Park, located in Fujiyoshida, Yamanashi, Japan, is a popular spot for cherry blossom viewing with breathtaking views of Mt. Fuji in the background. The park is known for its picturesque cherry blossoms that bloom in spring, drawing visitors from far and wide to admire the stunning scenery. Apart from its natural beauty, the park also holds cultural significance as a place of worship and pilgrimage.

Activity Information

  • Location: Fujiyoshida, Yamanashi, Japan
  • Season: Best time to visit is during cherry blossom season in spring
  • Recommended Time: Early morning or late afternoon for the best views of Mt. Fuji
  • Notes: The park can get crowded during peak cherry blossom season. Bring a camera for stunning photo opportunities.
  • Entry Fee: Free admission to the park

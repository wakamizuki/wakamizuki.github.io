Hakone Checkpoint

Overview

Hakone Checkpoint, or Hakone Sekisho, is a historical checkpoint located in Hakone, Japan. It served as an important checkpoint during the Edo period, controlling traffic along the Tokaido road. The checkpoint features traditional Japanese architecture and offers visitors a glimpse into Japan's feudal past. It is surrounded by beautiful natural scenery and is a popular tourist destination for history enthusiasts.

Yumegaoka Observatory – Panoramic Views of Hokkaido

Overview: A Hidden Gem for Nature Lovers

The Yumegaoka Observatory (夢ヶ丘展望台) is a tranquil spot in Hokkaido, perfect for those seeking sweeping countryside views and a peaceful escape. From the observation deck, you can see rolling hills, farmlands, and on clear days, distant mountains. Walking trails wind through the area, making it ideal for a relaxing stroll or a scenic picnic.

What Can You Do at Yumegaoka Observatory?

Enjoy the fresh air and take in the scenery from the observation deck. The area is known for its peaceful atmosphere, making it a favorite among locals for morning walks and sunset viewing. There are benches and rest spots along the trails, and the site is accessible for all ages.

  • Take panoramic photos of the countryside
  • Walk the gentle trails and enjoy seasonal flowers
  • Relax at rest areas and observation points
  • Spot local birds and wildlife
  • Enjoy a picnic with a view

How to Get to Yumegaoka Observatory (Last updated: May 25, 2025)

Note: Access by train is nearly impossible. You must go by car or taxi. Public transport is very limited.
Note: Walking from Takkobu Auto Campground to Yumegaoka Observatory takes about 30 minutes, but reaching the campground itself on foot takes approximately 90 minutes. Considering the return trip, accessing the observatory entirely on foot is not practical.

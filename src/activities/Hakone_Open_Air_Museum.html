Hakone Open Air Museum

Overview

Hakone Open Air Museum, located in the scenic town of Hakone, Japan, is a unique outdoor art museum known for its impressive collection of sculptures and artworks set amidst beautiful natural surroundings. Visitors can enjoy a leisurely stroll through the expansive grounds, encountering works by renowned artists like Henry Moore, Rodin, and Picasso. The museum's open-air design allows for a harmonious blend of art and nature, creating a serene and inspiring experience for art enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

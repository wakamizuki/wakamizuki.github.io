Arai-Sekisho (Checkpoint)- A Historical Checkpoint with Rich Heritage

Overview: Experience the Past at Arai-Sekisho

Arai-Sekisho, an Edo-period checkpoint, controlled the movement of people and goods along the Tokaido road. Located in Shizuoka Prefecture, it provides a glimpse into the past with its reconstructed buildings and exhibits. Visitors can explore the guardhouses and learn about the checkpoint's operations and importance in maintaining security for the shogunate.
Not sure what a Sekisho is? These historic checkpoints played a crucial role in controlling travel during Japan's feudal era. Learn more about their fascinating history and significance here.

Izusan Shrine

Overview

Izusan Shrine is a historic Shinto shrine located in Atami, Shizuoka, Japan. It holds cultural significance and features traditional Japanese architecture in a serene natural setting. Visitors can explore the shrine grounds, learn about its history, and experience the peaceful atmosphere that the shrine offers.

Activity Information

  • Location: Atami, Shizuoka, Japan
  • Visiting Hours: Open daily from morning to evening
  • Recommended Time to Visit: Spring for cherry blossoms, autumn for fall foliage
  • Notes: Respect the shrine's customs and etiquette. Photography may be restricted in certain areas.
  • Admission: Free entry; donations appreciated

Japan's rural regions are brimming with natural beauty and unique activities that offer a refreshing escape from the bustling cities.

Escape the Crowds

While vibrant, large cities like Tokyo and Osaka are vibrant and full of attractions, they can often be crowded with tourists. Exploring Japan’s countryside not only provides a chance to experience stunning landscapes and serene environments but also allows you to engage in authentic local activities away from the crowds.

Sagami Lake
MORI MORI

Sagami Lake MORI MORI offers a variety of outdoor activities surrounded by beautiful nature. Enjoy exciting high-rope courses, ziplining through the trees, and camping in scenic spots. It's a perfect destination for those seeking adventure and family-friendly fun.

Fuji-Q Highland

Fuji-Q Highland is renowned for its thrilling attractions set against the stunning backdrop of Mount Fuji. It features world-famous roller coasters like "FUJIYAMA," "Do-Dodonpa," and "Takabisha," making it a must-visit for thrill-seekers. The park also offers family-friendly rides and character shows, catering to visitors of all ages.

Hitachi Seaside Park

Hitachi Seaside Park is a vast national park located in Hitachinaka City, Ibaraki Prefecture. It is renowned for its stunning landscapes, with vibrant flowers blooming throughout the seasons. The park is especially famous for its blue nemophila flowers in spring and the red kochia bushes in autumn. Inside the park, you'll find cycling paths, an amusement park, and various attractions, making it a popular destination for families, couples, and tourists alike. Additionally, the park hosts a variety of events throughout the year, drawing visitors from all over to enjoy its expansive grounds.

Forest Adventure

Forest Adventure is a network of outdoor adventure parks across Japan, inspired by a concept that originated in France. These parks feature tree-top courses where participants wear harnesses to navigate through various obstacles such as climbing nets, rope bridges, and zip lines. The parks offer a thrilling experience set in natural surroundings, combining physical activity with fun. Suitable for families and groups of friends, Forest Adventure provides a unique way to enjoy nature and challenge oneself, with each location designed to integrate with its local environment.

Top 3 Countryside Day Trips from Tokyo – Easy Escapes into Nature and Culture

Looking to escape the city without venturing too far? These countryside spots are perfect for a relaxing day trip or overnight stay — all within 1–2 hours from central Tokyo.

1. Oshino Village – Mt. Fuji Views & Fresh Spring Water in Yamanashi

Nestled between lakes and mountains, Oshino Village offers a peaceful setting with traditional thatched-roof homes and clear spring water ponds fed by Mt. Fuji. Great for slow strolls and iconic photo spots.

Oshino Hakkai features eight crystal-clear ponds formed by Mt. Fuji’s snowmelt, surrounded by old-style houses—perfect for tranquil strolls and tasting fresh spring water. For a playful experience, Oshino Ninja Village lets you dress as a ninja, try shuriken throwing, and explore trick houses, making it fun for families and groups. The Shinnashogawa Promenade offers a scenic walking trail along a clear stream lined with flowers and cherry blossoms, ideal for Mt. Fuji reflection photos at sunrise or sunset. To deepen your understanding, the Mt. Fuji World Heritage Center (Oshino Annex) provides interactive exhibits and short films about the geology and culture of Mt. Fuji.

2. Hakone – Hot Springs, Art, and Mountain Scenery in Kanagawa

Hakone is one of Japan’s most beloved resort areas. Ride the ropeway, visit outdoor museums, soak in onsen, and admire views of Fuji across Lake Ashi — all just a short ride from Tokyo. Discover the perfect blend of nature and culture in Hakone and its surroundings: stroll the peaceful shores of Lake Ashi, visit the sacred Hakone Shrine, explore the dramatic volcanic landscapes of Owakudani, and enjoy open-air sculptures at the Hakone Open-Air Museum. This countryside getaway offers hot springs, scenic Mt. Fuji views, and a refreshing escape into Japan’s natural beauty.

3. Sayama & Tokorozawa – Tea Culture, Family Fun, and Local Discoveries in Saitama

West of Tokyo, the Sayama & Tokorozawa area mixes nature and entertainment. Watch a baseball game at Belluna Dome, explore Seibuen Amusement Park’s retro town, or picnic at the vast Aviation Park. Discover a unique day trip from Tokyo: travel from Shinjuku to the lush tea fields of Sayama, experience hands-on tea picking at Miyanoen Garden, and end your day with an exciting baseball game at Belluna Dome. This course blends rural charm, local flavors, and Japanese sports culture—all in one day.

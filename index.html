1. Oshino Village ‚Äì Mt. Fuji Views & Fresh Spring Water in Yamanashi

Nestled between lakes and mountains, Oshino Village offers a peaceful setting with traditional thatched-roof homes and clear spring water ponds fed by Mt. Fuji. Great for slow strolls and iconic photo spots.

Oshino Hakkai features eight crystal-clear ponds formed by Mt. Fuji‚Äôs snowmelt, surrounded by old-style houses‚Äîperfect for tranquil strolls and tasting fresh spring water. For a playful experience, Oshino Ninja Village lets you dress as a ninja, try shuriken throwing, and explore trick houses, making it fun for families and groups. To deepen your understanding, the Mt. Fuji World Heritage Center (Oshino Annex) provides interactive exhibits and short films about the geology and culture of Mt. Fuji.
